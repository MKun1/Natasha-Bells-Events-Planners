Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?
We recommend booking at least 1-2 months for all events which is usually sufficient. However, we can accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.
What areas in Zimbabwe do you serve?
We serve most major cities and towns across Zimbabwe including Harare, Marondera and Mutare . We also travel to rural areas for special events.
Do you offer custom event packages?
Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom packages tailored to your specific needs, budget, and vision. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we'll create a personalized proposal.
What types of events do you organize?
We organize a wide range of events including weddings, corporate conferences, birthday celebrations, cultural festivals, anniversary parties and charity events.
